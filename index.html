Game Rules


  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he/she whishes
  • Each result get added to his/her ROUND score
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his/her ROUND score gets lost
  • After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his/her ROUND score gets added to his/her TOTAL score
  • After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 or more points on TOTAL score wins the game
